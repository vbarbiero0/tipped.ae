BRAND IDENTITY · V1 · JULY 2026

tipped — brand guidelines

A platform for Dubai's street cats. The name and the mark come from the ear-tip — the clipped ear that says a street cat has been rescued, fixed and cared for. The clipped ear is always the point of color.

01 · The lockup
tipped
tipped
Rules. Always lowercase. Nunito ExtraBold (800). Ears centered on the second p, bases at the x-height. Left ear plain Sunset, right ear tipped Tip pink — never recolored, never swapped, on light or dark.
Geometry. Ear width ≈ 0.33× font-size, gap ≈ 0.07×, pair top ≈ 0.21× above the ascender. SVG paths: plain M8,44 L18,12 L28,44 Z · tipped M8,44 L12,24 L24,20 L28,44 Z, stroke 9 round-joined, ±12° rotation.
02 · The monogram
The head silhouette. Cream head with the brand ears filled in — plain Sunset left (curved like the logo), tipped Tip pink right — never recolored. Avatar = Cocoa circle, favicon = Cocoa rounded square (~27% radius). Use wherever the full wordmark won’t fit.

05 · Voice
Honest and direct about street life. No sob stories. Cats have names, histories and opinions — write them like the rescuer talks about them. Trust is stated plainly, not shouted: money for care goes to the clinic, purchases buy what rescuers actually need.
